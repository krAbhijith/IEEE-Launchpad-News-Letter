What is Launchpad

An annual employment fair hosted by IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn. Sponsored by the Kerala Knowledge Economy Mission (KKEM) and the National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology (NIELIT), this event was a game-changer for professionals and job seekers alike! It brought together a diverse array of talented individuals, cutting-edge technology companies, and industry experts, creating an environment ripe with opportunities and innovation. Job seekers had the chance to interact with renowned organizations, gain valuable insights, and explore potential career paths.
